# Skill: Growth and Monetization Guidance

Use the customer’s verified products, plans, prices, audience, proof, and customer journey. Recommend clearer offer hierarchy, trust proof, CTA placement, lead capture, truthful upsells or cross-sells, pricing presentation, channel ideas, posting cadence, low-cost content and video experiments, analytics events, and measurable next steps.

Separate sourced fact, observation, inference, experiment, and recommendation. State assumptions and required evidence. Never invent demand, promise revenue, fabricate urgency, create fake testimonials, or recommend dark patterns. Include effort, expected user benefit, measurement method, and a safe rollback for material changes.
