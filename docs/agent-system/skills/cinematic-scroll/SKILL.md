# Skill: Cinematic Scroll Production

1. Confirm three or four scenes and their exact order.
2. Create two coherent sequence options with two reference images per scene.
3. Obtain the customer’s selection through an expiring hashed link scoped to the correct project, option group, and revision round.
4. Estimate generation credits and stop above 150 credits for three scenes or 200 for four scenes unless a new approved budget exists.
5. Generate only through an authenticated provider connection after budget and rights approval.
6. Poll the same asynchronous job; do not duplicate it after a timeout or lost response.
7. Optimize media for reversible scrubbing, preserve full frames, contain touch gestures, and provide reduced-motion and manual fallbacks.
8. Verify desktop and mobile smoothness, decoder memory, first/final frame reachability, synchronized progress, normal page scrolling after touch release, and failure recovery.
