# Cinematic Scroll Site — $250 one-time scope

The Cinematic Scroll Site is a tightly bounded single-page motion experience. It is designed for a product, launch, campaign, portfolio, or service that benefits from one cinematic story controlled by the visitor's scroll.

## Included

- One responsive single-page microsite.
- One scroll-scrubbed motion sequence with up to four story beats.
- One primary call to action.
- Essential customer-provided copy and brand integration.
- AI-assisted media exploration using Higgsfield or a comparable provider when appropriate and available.
- Human review of the final sequence, claims, rights, responsive behavior, and accessibility fallbacks.
- Native scrolling; no fake scrollbar or required scroll-jacking.
- Desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts.
- Reduced-motion and lightweight mobile fallbacks.
- Deployment-ready source files.
- One consolidated revision round.
- Target final delivery within three business days after payment and complete intake.

## Motion and accessibility standard

The motion sequence must support the story rather than hide essential information inside a video. Important headings, copy, links, and calls to action remain real HTML. The implementation must provide keyboard-reachable controls where controls exist, readable contrast, a non-motion fallback, reduced-motion behavior, and a practical mobile experience.
